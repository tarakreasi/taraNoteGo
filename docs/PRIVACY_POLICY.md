# Privacy Policy

**TaraNote Privacy Policy**

*Effective Date: December 27, 2025*



## Introduction

Welcome to TaraNote ("we", "our", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use TaraNote.



## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Name
- Email address
- Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
- Optional: Username, Avatar, Hero Tagline, Hero Image

**Content You Create:**
- Notes and articles (title, content, excerpts)
- Notebooks/categories
- Cover images
- Publication dates

**Newsletter Subscriptions:**
- Email address (for newsletter signups)

### 1.2 Information Automatically Collected

**Usage Data:**
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring URL

**Cookies:**
- Session cookies (to keep you logged in)
- Preference cookies (to remember your theme preference)



## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- **Provide the Service**: Create and manage your account, store your notes
- **Authenticate**: Verify your identity when you log in
- **Communicate**: Send you newsletters (if subscribed), important service updates
- **Improve**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
- **Secure**: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
- **Legal**: Comply with legal obligations



## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data under the following legal bases:

- **Contract**: To fulfill our agreement to provide TaraNote services
- **Consent**: For newsletter subscriptions (you can withdraw consent anytime)
- **Legitimate Interest**: To improve our service and ensure security
- **Legal Obligation**: To comply with laws and regulations



## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT sell your personal data.

**We may share your data with:**

- **Service Providers**: Hosting providers (VPS), email services (if sending newsletters)
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law, court order, or government request
- **Business Transfers**: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

**Public Information:**
- Published articles are publicly accessible
- Your username and name are visible on published articles
- Your portfolio page (`/portfolio/{username}`) is public



## 5. Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Permanently deleted within 30 days of deletion request
- **Newsletter Subscriptions**: Retained until you unsubscribe
- **Usage Logs**: Retained for 90 days



## 6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have the right to:

### Access
Request a copy of your personal data.

**How**: Email us at ajarsinau@gmail.com with subject "Data Access Request"

### Rectification
Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

**How**: Update via Settings Panel or email us

### Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)
Request deletion of your data.

**How**: Delete your account via Settings or email us

### Data Portability
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

**How**: Email us for a JSON export of your notes

### Withdrawal of Consent
Unsubscribe from newsletters at any time.

**How**: Click "Unsubscribe" in any newsletter email (future feature) or email us

### Object to Processing
Object to certain types of processing (e.g., direct marketing).

**How**: Email ajarsinau@gmail.com



## 7. Children's Privacy

TaraNote is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.



## 8. Cookies and Tracking

### Essential Cookies
- **Session Cookie**: Keeps you logged in
- **CSRF Token**: Protects against cross-site request forgery

### Preference Cookies
- **Theme**: Remembers your light/dark mode preference

### Analytics (Optional)
- We may use analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Plausible) to understand usage
- You can opt out via browser settings or Do Not Track headers

**Cookie Lifespan**: Session cookies expire when you close your browser. Persistent cookies last up to 1 year.

**How to Disable**: Adjust your browser settings (note: disabling cookies may break functionality)



## 9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption**: HTTPS for data in transit, database encryption at rest
- **Access Control**: Strict access controls, role-based permissions
- **Password Hashing**: Bcrypt with high work factor
- **Regular Audits**: Security audits and penetration testing
- **Backups**: Encrypted daily backups stored securely

**However**, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
