# User Personas - TaraNote UI Design V6

This document defines the target users for TaraNote's "Living Digital Garden" ecosystem.



## Primary Personas

### 1. **Alex - The Technical Architect**
**Role:** Senior Developer / DevOps Engineer
**Goal:** Document complex systems and build a "Second Brain."

**Needs form TaraNote V6:**
- **Dual-View:** A private workspace for raw thinking vs. a polished public view for sharing.
- **Performance:** Auto-save < 1.5s and instant page loads.
- **Code Support:** Robust syntax highlighting in the editor.
- **Digital Garden:** A place to grow "Evergreen" notes over time.

**User Journey:**
1.  Drafts a rough architecture diagram in **TaraNote Workspace**.
2.  Refines it over a week, marking it as a "Budding" note.
3.  Once polished, hits "Publish" to move it to the **Digital Garden**.
4.  Shares the link on Twitter; recruiters see the professional **Hero Section** Alex customized in Settings.



### 2. **Jordan - The Portfolio Builder**
**Role:** Junior Developer / Job Seeker
**Goal:** Impress recruiters with a polished, personal brand.

**Needs form TaraNote V6:**
- **Visual Customization:** Uploads a professional **Avatar** and Cover Image.
- **Landing Page Control:** Customizes the "Welcome" hero text to pitch their skills.
- **No-Config SEO:** Articles automatically get slugified URLs (`/articles/my-first-react-app`).
- **Visual Customization:** Uploads a professional **Avatar** and Cover Image.
- **Landing Page Control:** Customizes the "Welcome" hero text to pitch their skills.
- **No-Config SEO:** Articles automatically get slugified URLs (`/articles/my-first-react-app`).
- **Minimalist Design:** The "Zen Glass" theme makes their content look premium out-of-the-box.

**User Journey:**
1.  Logs into Dashboard > **Settings Panel**.
2.  Uploads a new professional headshot (Avatar) and sets the "Job Title" to "Frontend Developer".
3.  Writes a "My Journey" article and pins it as **Featured**.
4.  Sends the `/portfolio/jordan-dev` link to hiring managers.



### 3. **Sarah - The Knowledge Curator**
**Role:** Product Manager / UX Researcher
**Goal:** Organize research and share insights selectively.

**Needs form TaraNote V6:**
- **Notebook Organization:** Keeps "User Research" separate from "Product Specs".
- **Search:** Quickly finds that one insight from last month.
- **Status Workflow:** Keeps rough drafts private while publishing only finished case studies.

## Product Positioning (UI Design V6)

**TaraNote is:**
> A **Living Digital Garden** for creators who want the speed of a notepad and the polish of a professional portfolio.

**Core Value Proposition:**
1.  **Write Private, Publish Public:** Seamless transition from thought to content.
2.  **Zero-Config Polish:** "Zen Glass" design system makes everyone look like a pro designer.
3.  **Your Identity:** Full control over Profile, Avatar, and Landing Page text.
